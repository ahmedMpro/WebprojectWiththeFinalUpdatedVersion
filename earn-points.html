How to Earn Swapify Points ⭐

Learn how the Swapify Points system works and how to maximize your earnings.

⭐

What are Swapify Points?

Swapify Points are a reward currency you earn by being an active member of the Swapify community. 100 Points = $1.00 in store credit. You can use points to get discounts at checkout on any deal.

How the System Works — Step by Step

1

Make a Purchase

Every dollar you spend earns you 10 Points. Buy any deal from the Half Price Deals section and watch your points grow automatically after payment is confirmed.

2

Check Your Wallet

Visit Profile → Wallet to see your current points balance and transaction history. Points are credited immediately after a successful payment.

3

Redeem at Checkout

On the payment page, find the Order Summary section. Check the "Apply available points" box to automatically apply your points as a discount. 100 points = $1 off your total.

4

Keep Earning!

There's no cap — the more you shop and swap, the more points you accumulate. Points never expire as long as your account remains active.
