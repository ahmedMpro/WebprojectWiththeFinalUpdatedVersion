Our Story

From a garage swap meet to a thriving global community.

It Started With a Camera 📷

In 2022, our founder Sarah was moving apartments and realized she had a vintage camera she no longer used. Instead of selling it online for a fraction of its value, she posted it in a local group saying: "I'll trade this for guitar lessons." Within hours, a local musician named Ahmed responded. That single swap sparked an idea.

Growing the Community 🌱

By 2023, the concept had spread to 5 cities. Users were swapping everything from Spanish lessons to furniture. The magic wasn't the items — it was the connections being built. Neighbors who'd never spoken were suddenly sharing coffee after a skill swap. That warmth became the core of what Swapify is today.

Today & Beyond 🚀

Swapify now serves 2,000+ members across 42 cities. We're building tools for trust, discovery, and community governance — so that every swap feels safe, fair, and joyful. Our goal: 1 million swaps by 2027.
