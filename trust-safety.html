Trust & Safety

Your safety is our top priority. Here's how we protect every swap.

✅

Verified Profiles

All members go through email verification. Optional ID verification unlocks a "Verified" badge and higher trust score.

⭐

Rating System

After every swap, both parties rate each other. Low-rated accounts are reviewed and may be restricted.

🔒

Secure Messaging

All chats happen in-app. We monitor for scam patterns and automatically flag suspicious conversations.

🚨

Report & Response

Our team reviews every report within 24 hours. Confirmed violations result in warnings, suspensions, or bans.

Safe Swap Tips

  • 🤝 Meet in public places for in-person swaps
  • 💬 Keep all communication inside Swapify until trust is established
  • 📸 Photograph items before and after a swap
  • 🚩 Report anything that feels wrong — no questions asked
  • ❌ Never send money in advance for a swap
