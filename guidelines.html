Community Guidelines

These rules keep our community kind, safe, and worth being part of.

✅ Be Honest

Accurately describe the condition of items and the level of your skills. Don't exaggerate, hide defects, or misrepresent what you're offering.

🤝 Respect Everyone

Treat every member with dignity. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language will result in permanent removal.

🔒 Protect Privacy

Never share personal information about other members without consent. Keep communications within the Swapify platform until trust is established.

⏰ Honour Your Commitments

If you agree to a swap, follow through. Repeated no-shows will affect your trust score and may lead to account suspension.

🚫 No Illegal Items

Do not list counterfeit goods, controlled substances, weapons, or anything else illegal in your jurisdiction. Violations are reported to authorities.
